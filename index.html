What We Do

Streetfix connects the community to local government to maintain a high quality urban environment. Streetfix provides a collaborative platform to local infrastructure inventory and management through crowdsourcing data. Community members are able to geotag poor conditions in vehicular and pedestrian environments in real time, including the states of roads and pedestrian paths, poor pedestrian connectivity, and barriers to visibility in driving.
